This model provides a comprehensive computer vision resource for the identification and classification of a wide array of international traffic signage. Featuring a **pre-trained object detection model** and a dataset of 10,000 images, this resource is capable of distinguishing between 29 distinct classes—including speed limits, directional instructions, and hazard warnings—making it a vital component for navigation and safety systems.

## Ways to Use Traffic and Road Signs Model

1. **Autonomous Vehicle Perception:** Integrate the model into self-driving car software to ensure real-time recognition of critical commands such as Stop signs, Give Way instructions, and speed limit changes.
2. **Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS):** Power in-car dashboard alerts that warn drivers about upcoming hazards like slippery roads, pedestrian crossings, or narrow lanes to improve road safety.
3. **Smart Infrastructure Auditing:** Deploy the model on municipal survey vehicles to automatically catalog and assess the condition of road signs across a city, ensuring all regulatory signage is present and visible.
4. **Intelligent Speed Assistance (ISA):** Use the model to dynamically update vehicle speed limiters by recognizing local speed signs (e.g., 20km/h or 50mph zones), helping drivers stay within legal limits.
5. **Real-time Traffic Mapping:** Enhance GPS and navigation apps by providing real-time visual verification of road rules, such as "No Entry" zones or "Truck Traffic Prohibited" areas, ensuring more accurate route planning.
